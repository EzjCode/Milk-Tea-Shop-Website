Our Story

About Freaky Milk Tea

Our Journey

Founded in 2020, Freaky Milk Tea started with a simple dream: to bring the authentic taste of premium bubble tea to Davao City. What began as a small passion project has grown into a beloved destination for tea enthusiasts.

Our founder, inspired by travels across Taiwan and Japan, wanted to recreate the magic of traditional milk tea while adding a unique Filipino twist. The name "Freaky" represents our bold approach to flavor combinations and our commitment to surprising our customers with exciting new creations.

Today, we proudly serve thousands of happy customers who share our love for quality bubble tea. Each drink is made fresh to order, using premium tea leaves imported from Taiwan and locally-sourced fresh milk.
